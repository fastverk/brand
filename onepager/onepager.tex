  \section*{The mark}
  Plays off \textbf{F}, \textbf{V}, the down-triangle $\forall$ and the horizontal
  arms $\exists$ — a deliberate golden-ratio construction on the unit circle
  ($\varphi = 0.618$), not a hand-traced drawing. One parametric source produces
  every asset: SVG, per-platform icons, the brandbook, slide decks, and office
  templates.

  \section*{Family}
  \textbf{full} — accent field + muted cut, for large sizes. \quad
  \textbf{lower} — accent arrow on a muted field, for small sizes.
