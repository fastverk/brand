% ── the mark ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{The mark}
The mark plays off \textbf{F}, \textbf{V}, the down-triangle \(\forall\)
(universal quantifier) and the horizontal arms \(\exists\)/\textsf{E} —
fitting, since Lean and its quantifiers do a lot of the work. It is a
deliberate construction on the unit circle and the golden ratio
(\(\varphi = 0.618\)), not a hand-traced drawing.

\begin{minipage}[c]{0.56\textwidth}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*,itemsep=1pt]
  \item Equilateral triangle on the unit circle, apex down \(\to T_{\mathrm{out}}\).
  \item Copy scaled to circumradius \(\varphi \to T_{\mathrm{in}}\); \(\mathrm{RING}=T_{\mathrm{out}}-T_{\mathrm{in}}\).
  \item Band thickness \(T=\tfrac12(1-\varphi)\) is the one gap/stroke unit.
  \item \textbf{Top-right cut} — the right-leg segment whose height equals its
        own base (\(2T/\sqrt3\)): a 60\(^\circ\) parallelogram.
  \item \textbf{Crossbar} — the F arm / \(\forall\) bar / \(\exists\) stroke, "/"-beveled.
  \item \textbf{Arrow cut} — a \(\perp\) cut through a chosen "\textbackslash" midpoint.
  \item \textbf{Accent + tertiary} fills complete the figure.
\end{enumerate}
\end{minipage}\hfill

  {\footnotesize unit circle · \(T_{\mathrm{out}}\) (black) · \(T_{\mathrm{in}}=\varphi\,T_{\mathrm{out}}\) (amber)}

\section*{Grid \& optical center}
A downward triangle is top-heavy: its mass (centroid) sits above its bounding-box
center. The mark is therefore placed on its \emph{optical} center
(\(c_y=-0.13\), vs. the bounding-box center \(-0.25\)) so it reads as centered.

\begin{center}
  \includegraphics[width=62mm]{grid.png}\\[1mm]
  {\footnotesize amber crosshair = the optical center the mark is balanced on}
\end{center}

% ── clear space ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{Clear space}
Keep clear space around the icon of at least the corner radius
(\(0.20\times\) the icon side, the amber unit below). The rounded-rect background
\emph{is} the icon; never crop or overlap it inside this margin.

\begin{center}
  \includegraphics[width=62mm]{clearspace.png}\\[1mm]
  {\footnotesize dashed = minimum clear space · amber square = the clearance unit}
\end{center}
